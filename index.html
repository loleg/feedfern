{feedfern}

a mashup created for #hackcamp 13.6.2010

This is a demonstration mashup that pulls together the public Google Buzz feed (easily customizable to use your own feed or search query), together with the latest news from Bloggers Against Hunger and displays it on top of your web site using floating windows. Fork the source and make it better here.

Click on the page banner to open it. Click again to close. Keep clicking. Double-clicking on any item that appears opens the link in a new window.

Thanks to the #hackcamp crew and all the sponsors, for a fantastic weekend of fun & code!

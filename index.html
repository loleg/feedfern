{feedfern}

a mashup created for #hackcamp 13.6.2010

This is a demonstration mashup that pulls together the public Google Buzz feed (easily customizable to use your own feed or search query), together with the latest news from Bloggers Against Hunger and displays it on top of your web site using floating windows. Check the bottom of the page to see how to edit the feed. Fork the source and make it better here.

Also check out Chip In, a mobile app that lets you give up just one simple, everyday luxury to help in the fight against hunger.

Made with: Buzz API, Bloggers Against Hunger feeds, jQuery 1.4, MagpieRSS, PHP, HTML5, CSS3, gEdit, GIMP, Ubuntu Linux.

Thanks to the #hackcamp crew and all the sponsors, for a fantastic weekend!
